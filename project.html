Our Mission

We thrive to provide loving homes to animals in need. Some of our main values include compassion, dedication, empathy, love, and integrity. Our mission is to enrich the lives of both the animals and people throughout our community. We seek to end pet homelessness, promote the health of the animals, and develope a human-animal connection. The main goal is to provide a home to every pet.

Choose the


  • Stray

  • Schuck-Stripling Animal Center warmly welcomes stray/found pets in the City of Knoxvill. If you have found a pet in the city, please bring it to our Lake Loudon address between the hours of 10a.m-5 p.m. Find out more information in our link below.

  • Lost and Found

  • If you have lost a pet, visit the Pet Resource Center(information included in the link below).We are open seven days a week from 10a.m- 4p.m. Check every other day to find your lost pet. Reach out to local vets to see if your pet was found there. Contact all other shelters. *Please be aware fees may apply to reclaim your lost pet due to madated laws.

  • Pet Resource Center and Surrender

  • 50GB Before giving up your pet, please consider other options as takign your pet to any animal shelter should be the last resort. We accept stray/lost pets by appointment only.
